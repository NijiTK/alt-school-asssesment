How to make Amala, Ewedu and Gbegiri

This is a popular Nigerian dish from the Yoruba people. It is very delicious and healthy.

Prep time: 30 mins
Cook time: 45 mins
Servings: 3
Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Yam Flour (Elubo)
  • 1 cup peeled beans
  • Fresh Ewedu leaves
  • 1 spoon of Iru
  • Palm oil
  • Crayfish and seasoning

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil the beans until they are very soft and blend them for the Gbegiri.
  2. Cook the blended beans with palm oil and pepper.
  3. Boil Ewedu leaves with a little potash and whisk until it is smooth.
  4. Boil water and mix the yam flour quickly so it does not have lumps.
  5. Serve the hot Amala with the Gbegiri and Ewedu soup.

Tips

Use a wooden spoon to turn the Amala well so it becomes very smooth.

Nutrition Facts

This meal has about 450 calories and is high in fiber.
