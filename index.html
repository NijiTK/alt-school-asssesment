Homemade Amala, Ewedu and Gbegiri

This is my favorite Nigerian dish. It is made from yam flour and served with two types of soup.

Prep: 30 mins | Cook: 45 mins | Servings: 3

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Elubo
  • 1 cup beans
  • Ewedu leaves
  • Locust beans (Iru)
  • Palm oil
  • Salt and seasoning

How to Cook

  1. Boil beans until very soft for Gbegiri.
  2. Blend the beans and cook with palm oil.
  3. Cook Ewedu leaves with a little potash.
  4. Boil water and mix the flour fast.
  5. Serve everything hot.

Notes

Make sure there are no lumps in the Amala!

Nutrition

About 450 calories per plate.
